Understanding India's Energy Landscape

India's energy sector stands at the intersection of economic growth, environmental sustainability, and social development. As the world's third-largest energy consumer, India's journey towards energy security and transition to clean energy sources is crucial for global climate action. Government portals and analytical tools play a vital role in this transformation by providing transparent access to energy data, enabling informed decision-making for policymakers, researchers, and industry stakeholders.
